Rules

Before everyone starts rioting, we have no rules that intercept with vanilla gameplay, griefing, killing, stealing, everything is allowed.

But there must be some rules to maintain the servers intentions and purpose. And those rules are for hacking and lagging the server

- It is not allowed to hack or use exploits on this server

- You must follow the Mojang and Microsoft EULA that you have agreed to when creating your account.

- Dont spam chat

- Do not be a part of any harassment, bullying or threats. This is a videogame...

- Do not make machines or do anything with the intention of lagging the server



Your playing privileges on the server may be deducted

Your accunt may be banned from the server

Your accunt may be muted from the chat

Remember to have fun, if this server doesn't suit you, there are many others to fill your needs. But in this server we play vanilla, no cheats, with no in-game rules
