About

Small norwegian community server with roots back to 1.15!

This server started with some twitch streamers that made a collab. Made this world we are playing on today and streamed together. LD's SMP values the integrity of the world and therefore will never wipe the server.

This page is not for recruiting or advertisement for the server, and not for people to join. This is mainly a site for the server and where we update the world archives and history, news and maintenance updates.


Check out our instagram! We have some posts from our modded server too, but when there are any mods we mention it in the post. Mostly pictures from the LD SMP Vanilla server!
